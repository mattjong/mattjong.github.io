Mindless, Meaningless and Complacent.

These are only three of the many characteristics that show the rise of the low-bass, low-tempo yet mesmerizing music known as tropical house. House originated from disc jockeys and music producers from Chicago's underground club culture, and features hi-hats, snare-drums, synthesizers, sampled vocals and riffs. Nowadays, the genre is popularized by Kygo, Matoma and Seeb to name a few. Now's the time to explore this popular genre. Welcome to paradise, the party has just begun.
