FIND YOUR NEXT TROPICAL HOUSE CONCERT

Shrine Auditorium is a mere five minute walk from USC campus and an excellent place to discover your first tropical house concert. The concerts here are a little more packed, but you definitely feel the energy within the crowd. You have a better chance of crowdsurfing, or being in the mosh pit as well. In 2020, there will be two concerts happening in February.

EKALI
FEB 14, 2020

MATOMA
FEB 21, 2020


To get to Shrine: Walk from the USC Village in a straight line down Jefferson. If you live near campus, fryft is also an option.
The Hollywood Palladium is a little further from USC campus but also features lively nightlife around its surroundings. Its a good place to grab some food near the Walk of Fame and then rally together to see a set. The concerts there are grander e place is a little bigger than the Shrine, so you can go with a bigger group as well. The DJ's perfomring here aren't nessecarily Tropical House, but use a lot of orchestral elements which make it a great experience.

ARMIN VAN BUREN
FEB 7, 2020

KSHMR

To get to Hollywood Palladium: You can take the metro and get off the Hollywood sign, or you could also take an Uber with all your friends there. At rush hour, Ubers could be expensive so I usually would go earlier at 3-4pm, grab some food and hang out in the area till show time.
