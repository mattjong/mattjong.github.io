"Stargazing" features the vocals of Justin Jesso and Kygo, a Tropical House DJ. It was released as the third single track on Kygo's EP after "It Ain't Me" and "First Time". The video follows a little boy's journey after losing his father suddenly due to a heart attack. After finding a note after his death saying "You will always find me in the stars" the boy was determined to build a rocket to meet his father. The peak of the song occurs in the chrous when the mother employs the help of other individuals in the neighbourhood to "light up the sky" and accomplish her son's mission to chase his dad. In this process, it helped both the mother and son to find healing. As this song peaked 11th on the Billboard charts, it also marked a change in the Tropical House scene as it utilized more classical melodic tones to create a harmony. Personally one of my favorite DJ's, Matoma is a Norweigan DJ and producer born in Norway, and is most known for his synth, scandinavian sounds in his work. He began by remixing Eminem and rap songs by the Notorious B.I.G. His debut album, Hakuna Matoma peaked at #8 on the Billboard 100's, and now has notable singles such as "Running Out" (featured above) and Sunday Morning which allows you to kick back.

Felix Jaehn was born in Germany and is a DJ that also specializes in Tropical House. He incorporates a lot of violin and strings influence in his music, specifically in the song "Ain't Nobody (Loves Me Better)", which is a classic re-work that was originally sung by Chaka Khan and topped the German singles chart, as well as billboard top 100. He has been featured in insomniac events as a DJ that's one to watch.
